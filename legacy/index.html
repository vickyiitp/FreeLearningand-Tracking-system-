Monetization Masterclass

The 100-Day Earning Challenge.

Stop just learning to code. Learn how to convert code into cash. This is the exact blueprint to monetize your skills using AI productivity and content creation.

01

Days 1-30: Build in Public

Do not hide your code. Create a Twitter/X and LinkedIn profile. Every day, post 1 technical challenge you solved using AI. Share the prompt and the code. Goal: Grow an audience of 1,000 developers and founders.

02

Days 31-60: Micro-Tooling

Identify a painful task (e.g., writing cover letters, summarizing PDFs). Build a single-page web app using React and the OpenAI API. Launch it for free to your new audience to build trust and gather emails.

03

Days 61-100: B2B Outreach

Use your free tool as a portfolio piece. Reach out to 10 local businesses or digital agencies a day. Offer to build them a custom AI chatbot or automate their data entry for $500 - $2000. Goal: Land 3 paying clients.
